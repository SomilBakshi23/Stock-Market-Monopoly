Basic Rules

  • Buy and sell stocks using real-time market data
  • Track your portfolio performance
  • Make strategic decisions based on market trends
  • Compete with other players to build the most valuable portfolio

How to Trade

  1. Search Stocks: Use the search function to find stocks by their symbol or company name
  2. Analyze: Study the stock charts and company information provided
  3. Buy Stocks: Click the "Buy" button and enter the number of shares you want to purchase
  4. Sell Stocks: View your portfolio and click "Sell" when you want to exit a position
  5. Track Performance: Monitor your gains and losses in the Portfolio section

Tips for Success

  • Diversify your portfolio to minimize risk
  • Keep track of market news and trends
  • Don't invest all your money in a single stock
  • Use the analysis tools provided to make informed decisions
  • Practice with small investments before making larger trades
